Top 5 Hidden Beaches in Thailand

By Pulkit Saxena · Sep 24, 2025

If you think Thailand is only about Phuket and Phi Phi Islands, think again. Beyond the tourist crowds lie untouched beaches where turquoise waters meet golden sands — and hardly a soul around. As a travel writer, my goal is to take you beyond the guidebooks and into secret escapes.

1. Sunrise Beach — Koh Lipe

Imagine waking up to the sound of waves gently brushing the shore while the first rays of sunlight paint the horizon. Sunrise Beach in Koh Lipe is perfect for snorkeling and morning swims.

2. Ao Manao — Prachuap Khiri Khan

A calm bay with powdery sand and fewer tourists — great for peaceful walks and local markets nearby.

3. Koh Yao Noi — quiet coves

Off the beaten track with small guesthouses and authentic food stalls.

4. Koh Kood — Klong Chao

Lush island, long beaches and clear shallow water for kids and snorkelers.

5. Railay West (early morning)

Visit before day-trippers arrive to enjoy dramatic limestone cliffs with near-empty shores.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit: November–March
  • How to Get There: Check local ferries and low-cost flights depending on which island you choose.
  • What to Pack: Snorkeling gear, reef-safe sunscreen, power bank, light rain jacket

These beaches reward travelers who prioritize calm, nature and local interactions over tourist amenities.
