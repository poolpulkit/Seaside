Packing list for a beach trip

By Pulkit Saxena · Sep 24, 2025

Use this checklist to pack light but safe for a multi-day beach trip.

Essentials

  • Passport / ID & photocopies
  • Cash and cards in waterproof pouch
  • Travel insurance details

Clothing & Beach

  • Swimsuits (2)
  • Light shirts, shorts, sarong
  • Flip-flops and water shoes

Health & Safety

  • Reef-safe sunscreen
  • Basic first-aid kit
  • Anti-diarrheal & any personal meds

Tech & Extras

  • Power bank, phone, waterproof case
  • Travel towel, reusable water bottle

Print this list and tick items as you pack — happy travels!
