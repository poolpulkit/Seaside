How to get from Bangkok to Koh Lipe

By Pulkit Saxena · Sep 24, 2025

There are several routes — choose based on budget and time.

Option 1 — Fly + Ferry

Fly from Bangkok (DMK or BKK) to Hat Yai or Trang, then take a taxi/minivan to the pier and hop a ferry to Koh Lipe.

Option 2 — Train + Bus + Ferry

Overnight train to Hat Yai, then transfer to a minivan and ferry — slower but scenic and cheaper.

Booking Tips

  • Buy ferry tickets in advance during high season.
  • Allow cushion time for transfers; roads and ferries can be delayed.
